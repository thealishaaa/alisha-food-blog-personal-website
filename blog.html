    Svart Cafe

    Do you look for aesthetic cafe that also serve Western food? I think Svart Cafe will be the place you desire to! It is located at Tamarind Square, Cyberjaya.

                 

    The most popular menu here is Spaghetti Buttermilk Chicken, which is a delightful fusion dish featuring tender, marinated chicken pieces seared to golden perfection, nestled in a rich and creamy buttermilk sauce. The sauce, infused with garlic, butter, and a touch of chili, wraps around spaghetti, creating a harmonious blend of flavours and textures. Topped with freshly grated Parmesan and a sprinkle of parsley, this dish offers a comforting and indulgent experience that’s perfect for any occasion.

                 

    Step into their aesthetic cafe, where every corner is designed to be a visual delight. Bathed in natural light streaming through large windows, the space is adorned with chic, minimalist decor. Soft pastel hues and elegant wooden accents create a warm, inviting atmosphere. Cozy seating arrangements, lush greenery, and artful displays add to the charm, making it the perfect backdrop for your photos. Whether you are capturing the intricate latte art, the stylish interior, or your own moments of relaxation, their cafe offers a picturesque setting that elevates every snapshot.
